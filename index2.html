Multi Policy Discount – Save up to 10%

Since many Kiwis have multiple cars, we offer a multi policy discount of up to 10% when you purchase two or more policies from Cove. This could be two cars, a car and a dog, or a dog and a cat – any combination of multiple insurance policies automatically qualifies you for our multi policy discount. T&Cs Apply.

There are more ways to save on insurance with Cove.

Multi policy discount isn’t the only way to save. Here are a few other options…

Pay annually – Save up to 10%

Here at Cove we offer up to a 10% discount on your insurance premium if you choose to pay annually. This is automatically applied when getting a quote online.

Refer a friend – Save up to $100

We believe we have service so good, you’ll want to tell your mates. A lot of Cove customers are now referring their friends & family to Cove, and each time they do, they get a free month of insurance worth up to $100. Once you join Cove, you can also begin to refer your mates from your MyCove dashboard.
